Astrology


History of the astrology

Birth Chart example.

The astrology is marked by superstition involving the stars. It has existed for thousands of years and it was developed differently by different folks. The oldest involving a horoscope system is the Chinese astrology. There are also records of Vedic astrology and Mayan astrology, and perhaps there was the presence of astrology in the culture of several other folks. Astrology involving the constellations of the zodiac is the most known today.


The Signs

In astrology, the zodiac is divided into 12 constellations, and the constellation whose Sun is passing on the day of your birth, marks your Sun sign, which defines your main personality traits. In general, these signs are based on the position of the stars at the time of their birth, and the map that records these positions, also showing the constellations of the zodiac involved, is called birth chart. All signs are represented by each constellation of the zodiac, being separated into twelve:

> Aquarius - January 20 | February 18
> Pisces - February 19 | March 20
> Aries - March 21 | April 19
> Taurus - April 20 | May 20
> Gemini - May 21 | June 20
> Cancer - June 21 | July 22
> Leo - July 23 | August 22
> Virgo - August 23 | September 22
> Libra - September 23 | October 22
> Scorpio - October 23 | November 23
> Sagittarius - November 22 | December 21
> Capricorn - December 22 | January 19
